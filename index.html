Aritra Bhattacharya

I am currently a postdoc at Beijing International Center for Mathematical Research, Peking University. I completed my Ph.D. in mathematics from the Institute of Mathematical Sciences, Chennai, India in April 2024, my Ph.D. supervisor was Prof. S. Viswanath.

My main research interest is in algebraic combinatorics, in particular I have worked on symmetric functions and Macdonald polynomials.

I am also interested in representation theory, in particular of Kac-Moody algebras and associated objects e.g, quantum groups and vertex algebras.
